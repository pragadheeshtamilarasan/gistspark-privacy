Simple Summary: GistSpark doesn't collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Your API keys stay on your device, and we don't track you.

Information We Don't Collect

GistSpark is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share:

  • Personal information or identifiers
  • Browsing history or website data
  • API keys or credentials
  • Content you summarize
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Cookies or similar tracking technologies

How Your Data is Handled

API Keys: Your API keys are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's secure storage. They never leave your device except to authenticate with your chosen AI provider (OpenAI, etc.).

Content Processing: When you summarize content, the webpage text is sent directly from your browser to your chosen AI provider (OpenAI, Groq, local LLM, etc.). GistSpark acts as a passthrough and never stores or processes this content.

Local Storage Only: All extension settings and preferences are stored locally on your device using Chrome's sync storage.

Third-Party Services

GistSpark integrates with AI providers you choose (OpenAI, Groq, local LLMs). When you use these services:

  • Content is sent directly to your chosen provider for processing
  • Each provider has their own privacy policy
  • GistSpark doesn't control how these providers handle data
  • You can use local LLMs for complete privacy

No Analytics or Tracking

GistSpark does not use:

  • Google Analytics or similar services
  • Error tracking or crash reporting
  • Usage metrics or behavioral analysis
  • Advertising networks or trackers

Children's Privacy

GistSpark does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we don't collect any personal information from users of any age, this policy applies universally.

Data Security

Since we don't collect or store personal data, there's no central database that could be compromised. Your API keys are secured by Chrome's built-in encryption and sync mechanisms.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we'll post the new version here and update the "Last Updated" date below. Since we don't collect contact information, we can't notify users directly of changes.
